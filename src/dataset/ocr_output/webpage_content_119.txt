1.         Granted. 
 
2.         Granted subject to all just exceptions. 
 
2&3.    The applicant has sought bail in FIR No. 63/2013
registered at Police Station Soldier Bazar, Karachi under section 489-F PPC. It
is contended by learned counsel for the applicant that the applicant has
falsely been involved in this case due to enmity with the complainant. It is
next contended that father of the applicant was having some business
transactions with the complainant party. It is further contended by learned
counsel for the applicant that the applicant has not been previously involved
in any criminal case or proceedings and the offence does not fall within
prohibitory clause of section 497 Cr.P.C. 
 
Be
that as it may, without adverting to the merits/demerits of the case, the
applicant is admitted to interim pre-arrest bail subject to his furnishing
surety in the sum of Rs.1,00,000/- (Rupees one lac only) and P.R. in the like
amount to the satisfaction of Nazir of this Court. 
 
            To come up for confirmation or otherwise on 17.09.2013,
for which date notice be also issued to Prosecutor General, Sindh and
complainant.
